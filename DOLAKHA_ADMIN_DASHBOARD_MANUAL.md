# 🏢 Dolakha Furniture - Admin Dashboard Manual

Welcome to the **Admin Dashboard Manual**! While Sanity CMS is used for changing website content (like adding products or banners), the **Admin Dashboard** is your daily operational control center. 

This dashboard is built directly into your website and is designed for fast, real-time management of your day-to-step operations: handling orders, talking to customers, and managing inventory.

This manual is written in clear, simple language so any staff member can comfortably operate the system.

## 1. Getting Started

### Accessing the Dashboard
You can access the Admin Dashboard by logging into the main website with your staff account and navigating to `/admin` (e.g., `www.dolakhafurniture.com/admin`). 

### Security & Roles
The dashboard is strictly locked down. Only accounts marked as **Admin** or **Staff** in the system can access this area. If a regular customer tries to access it, they will be automatically redirected back to the homepage.

---

## 2. Order Management

The **Orders** page is where you will spend most of your time. Every time a customer completes a checkout using the "Zero-G Checkout" on the website, the order instantly appears here.

### Viewing Orders
- You will see a list of all incoming orders, sorted by the newest first.
- Each order card displays the customer's name, their chosen delivery district, the total amount, and the items they purchased.

### Processing an Order
1. **Status Tracking:** You can track the lifecycle of an order from "New" to "Processing" to "Delivered".
2. **Order Details:** Clicking on an order gives you the exact delivery address, the customer's phone number, and any discount vouchers they used.
3. **Payment Method:** The order will clearly state if the customer wants to pay via **Cash on Delivery**, **Bank Transfer**, or if they need you to bring exact change.

---

## 3. Inquiries & Customer Support

When a customer uses the "Quick Connect Hub" or fills out the form on the website, it arrives in the **Inquiries** tab.

### Handling Support Tickets
- **General Inquiries:** Questions about delivery times, materials, or store hours.
- **Order Inquiries:** If a customer asks a question about an order they already placed, the system will automatically attach their **Order ID** to the inquiry. This means you can look up their exact order before you even call them back!
- **Fast Actions:** You can mark inquiries as "Resolved" once you have contacted the customer, helping you keep your inbox clean.

---

## 4. Sales Leads

The **Leads** tab is slightly different from Inquiries. While Inquiries are usually support questions, Leads are potential sales opportunities.

- **Bulk & Corporate Leads:** If a business wants to order 50 chairs for an office, it will appear here.
- **Custom Furniture Requests:** If a customer submits a request to build a custom-sized wardrobe or a specific sofa design, you will manage those high-value requests in this tab.
- *Best Practice:* Always try to contact Leads within 24 hours, as these are highly intent-driven customers.

---

## 5. Inventory Overview

The **Inventory** tab provides a fast, bird's-eye view of your stock without needing to log into Sanity CMS.

- **Quick Checks:** If a customer calls asking if the "Classic Wooden Bed" is in stock, you can quickly search for it here.
- **Stock Levels:** You can view which items are active, which are out of stock, and which items are currently hidden from the public website.

---

## 6. User Management

The **Users** tab is your customer database. 

- **Customer Profiles:** Every customer who creates an account on your website is listed here.
- **Order History:** You can click on a user to see their past purchase history. This is great for offering VIP discounts or helping a customer who forgot what they bought last year.
- **Staff Access:** Administrators can use this tab to grant "Staff" access to new employees, allowing them to log into this dashboard.

---

## 7. System Logs

The **Logs** tab is your digital security camera and system heartbeat. 

- **Automated Intelligence:** The system uses "Pulse Protocols" to track important events. If an error happens, or if a discount voucher fails, it is logged here.
- **Facebook Meta Pixel Events:** You can verify that your Facebook ads are tracking properly by watching the logs for "Add to Cart" or "Purchase" events.
- *Note:* Regular staff usually do not need to check this tab. It is primarily used by Administrators or IT support to ensure the website is running smoothly.

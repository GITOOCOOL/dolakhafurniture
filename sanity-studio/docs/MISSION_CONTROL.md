# 🕹️ Mission Control: The Sanity Operator's Guide
## Managing your Global Content Empire

The **Social Hub Dashboard** is where you prepare, schedule, and launch your broadcasts across the world.

---

### 🚀 1. Setting up a Mission
When you create a "Social Media" document in Sanity:
1.  **Select Media Type**: Toggle between **Video** (for Reels/Stories) or **Photo** (for static stories).
2.  **Define Placement**: Choose if this is intended for a **Feed Post**, **Story**, or **Reel**.
3.  **Hearthbeat Schedule**:
    *   `⚡️ Fast-Track`: Fires almost instantly (for testing).
    *   `Weekly/Daily`: Balanced distribution to avoid spam filters.
    *   `Manual Only`: Only fires when you hit the button.

### 📡 2. Selecting Targets
In the **Distribution & Deployment** section:
- Check the box for every platform you want to reach.
- You can mix **Facebook**, **Instagram**, and the **Website** in a single mission.

### 🏹 3. The Tactical Launch
There are two ways to deploy:
1.  **Automated**: Just set your schedule to "Active" and walk away. The bot will handle the rest.
2.  **Manual (The Launch Button)**:
    *   Click **"INITIATE TACTICAL LAUNCH"** for an instant broadcast.
    *   Use this for breaking news or time-sensitive furniture drops.

### 💡 4. Best Practices
*   **Safety First**: Don't post the same image more than 3 times a day to avoid being flagged by Meta.
*   **Instagram First**: We recommend always including Instagram in your missions, as it provides the cleanest "Story" integration.
*   **The Website Target**: Always keep the "Website" target checked to keep your brand's digital presence fresh!
